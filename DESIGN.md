# Design DNA — Bright Nest Learning

## Visual Personality: playful-creative
## Layout: product-led
## Typography: playful-rounded

### Color Palette
- Primary: #E8533A (coral-red) → oklch(0.60 0.18 25)
- Accent: #F5C842 (sunshine yellow) → oklch(0.85 0.17 85)
- Teal: #3ABFB8 → oklch(0.72 0.12 185)
- Navy: #1D2B4F → oklch(0.22 0.07 255)
- Surface: #FFF8F0 (warm cream-white) → oklch(0.98 0.012 60)
- Text dark: oklch(0.22 0.07 255) — deep navy

### Font Pairing
- Display: Fredoka — weight 400–700 (bold, friendly, rounded)
- Body: DM Sans — weight 300–500 (clean, legible, warm)

### Unique Design Choices
1. **Coral hero section** — The entire hero uses a warm coral-to-yellow radial gradient with noise texture overlay, completely breaking from the beige/cream competitor landscape. The hero text is white/cream on a vibrant coral background.
2. **Wiggle-edged section dividers** — SVG wave shapes between major sections to echo the playful, hand-crafted illustration aesthetic of children's educational materials.
3. **Colorful sticker-badge product cards** — Product cards feature bright circular emoji badges that overlap the card corners, creating layered sticker-book energy.
4. **Three-color section rhythm** — The page alternates: coral hero → warm cream → teal feature band → warm cream → yellow CTA band → navy footer. Each section has its own color identity, unlike any competitor.
5. **Oversized rounded display type** — Headlines at text-6xl/7xl with Fredoka, giving a fun-book cover feel that screams joy, not utility.
